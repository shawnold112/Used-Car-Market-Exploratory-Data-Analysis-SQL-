## Introduction 


This is my used-car-market exploratory data analysis project! This is my first SQL project using a  dataset from kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/austinreese/craigslist-carstrucks-data).

The dataset intially consisted of data that was too much to store on github, so it was condensed to make things more understandable to whomever may view it. 

### Key Features 

1. 'id': = The id of the car

2. 'price': The price of the car

3. 'year': Year car was made

4. car_long: Long of the car

5. 'lat': Lat of the car

6. 'vin': Vin of the car

7. 'car_condition: The condition of the car(good/bad/excellent)

9. 'model': The model of the car

10. 'manufacturer": The brand of the car

11. 'car_type": What type is the car

12. 'region': region of the car

13. 'cylinders': how many cylinders the car have

14. 'odometers': The car's odometer
